<h2>How to Use</h2>
<ol>

  <li>Set <strong>Size</strong> (1–128 characters, default 21).</li>
  <li>Choose an <strong>Alphabet</strong> preset, or select <em>Custom</em> to enter your own characters.</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Generate</strong>.</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Copy</strong> on any row to copy a single ID, or <strong>Copy All</strong> to copy all as newline-separated text.</li>
</ol>
