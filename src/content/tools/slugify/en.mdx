<h2>How to Use</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Type or paste your text into the input box.</li>
  <li>Choose your preferred separator (hyphen, underscore, or dot).</li>
  <li>Toggle Lowercase and Trim options as needed.</li>
  <li>The slug updates in real time — click <strong>Copy</strong> to copy it.</li>
</ol>

<h2>When to Use Slugs</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Blog post URLs:</strong> <code>/blog/how-to-use-slugs</code></li>
  <li><strong>Product pages:</strong> <code>/products/red-running-shoes</code></li>
  <li><strong>File names:</strong> <code>my-important-document.pdf</code></li>
  <li><strong>Database keys:</strong> Unique, human-readable identifiers without spaces.</li>
</ul>
