<h2>About Color Formats</h2>
<p>
  <strong>HEX</strong> is the most common format for the web. A six-digit code like
  <code>#1a73e8</code> encodes red, green, and blue channels as two hexadecimal digits each (00–FF).
</p>
<p>
  <strong>RGB</strong> expresses the same channels as decimal numbers (0–255), making it easier to
  adjust values programmatically: <code>rgb(26, 115, 232)</code>.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>HSL</strong> stands for Hue, Saturation, Lightness — a human-readable model.
  Hue is 0–360°, saturation and lightness are percentages: <code>hsl(217, 80%, 51%)</code>.
  HSL is ideal for generating color scales by varying lightness.
</p>
