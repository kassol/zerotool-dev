<h2>How to Use</h2>
<ol>

  <li>Use the native color picker, type a hex value, or paste from your design tool. Three-digit shorthand (<code>#abc</code>) is auto-expanded.</li>

  <li>If body text AA (4.5 : 1) fails, a suggestion card appears with a one-click <strong>Apply suggestion</strong> action that adjusts the foreground lightness while keeping the same hue.</li>
</ol>

<h2>WCAG Contrast Ratio Explained</h2>
<p>The WCAG 2.x contrast ratio is computed from the <em>relative luminance</em> of two colors. Each sRGB channel is gamma-decoded with the piecewise transfer curve, weighted by <code>0.2126·R + 0.7152·G + 0.0722·B</code>, and the ratio is <code>(L1 + 0.05) / (L2 + 0.05)</code> where L1 is the lighter of the two luminances. The result lies between 1 (no contrast) and 21 (pure black on pure white).</p>

<h2>Body, Large, UI — Three Tiers</h2>
<p>WCAG 2 splits contrast targets by use case:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Body text (Success Criterion 1.4.3)</strong> — at least <code>4.5 : 1</code> for AA and <code>7 : 1</code> for AAA. Counts text below 18&nbsp;px regular or below 14&nbsp;px bold.</li>
  <li><strong>Large text</strong> — at least <code>3 : 1</code> for AA and <code>4.5 : 1</code> for AAA. Counts text 18.66&nbsp;px bold or larger, or 24&nbsp;px regular and larger.</li>
  <li><strong>UI components and graphical objects (Success Criterion 1.4.11)</strong> — at least <code>3 : 1</code>. Covers borders, focus indicators, icons, chart strokes, and similar non-text visual elements. WCAG 2 does not specify an AAA target here.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why "Keep Hue, Adjust Lightness"</h2>
<p>Brand colors and design tokens are usually picked for hue identity, not luminance. When contrast fails, blindly switching to black or white can break the visual language. This tool walks the foreground along its OKLCH lightness axis (perceptually uniform), keeps the original hue and chroma, and stops at the first lightness that satisfies <code>4.5 : 1</code> against the current background. The result is the color closest to your original that still passes body AA. If your hue cannot reach the target within the sRGB gamut, the tool falls back to flat black or white.</p>
