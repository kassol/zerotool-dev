<h2>How to Use</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Paste your CSV data into the <strong>CSV Input</strong> textarea, or click <strong>Upload CSV</strong> to load a file.</li>
  <li>Select your <strong>SQL Dialect</strong> (MySQL, PostgreSQL, or SQLite).</li>
  <li>Enter a <strong>Table Name</strong>. The default is <code>my_table</code>.</li>
  <li>Choose <strong>Batch INSERT</strong> (one statement, multiple rows) or <strong>Individual INSERT</strong> (one statement per row).</li>
  <li>The SQL output updates instantly. Click <strong>Copy</strong> to copy it to your clipboard.</li>
</ol>

<h2>NULL Handling</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Empty CSV fields produce <code>NULL</code> (unquoted) in the SQL output.</li>
  <li>Fields that contain only whitespace are treated as strings, not NULL.</li>
  <li>Pure numeric values (integers and decimals) are inserted without quotes, so they are stored as numbers rather than strings.</li>
  <li>All other values are wrapped in single quotes with internal single quotes escaped as <code>''</code>.</li>
</ul>
