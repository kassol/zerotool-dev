<h2>How to Use</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Paste or type your text into the <strong>Input Lines</strong> textarea — one item per line.</li>
  <li>Click any action button: <strong>Remove Duplicates</strong> (unique lines), <strong>Sort A→Z / Z→A</strong> (alphabetical), <strong>Sort Numeric</strong> (by leading number), <strong>Reverse</strong> (flip order), <strong>Shuffle</strong> (randomize), <strong>Trim Lines</strong> (strip whitespace), or <strong>Remove Empty</strong> (delete blank lines).</li>
  <li>The result appears in the <strong>Output</strong> textarea. Click <strong>Copy</strong> to copy it to your clipboard.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Worked Examples</h2>

<h3>Remove duplicates from a list</h3>
<p>Input:</p>
<pre><code>apple
banana
cherry
apple
banana
date</code></pre>
<p>After clicking <strong>Remove Duplicates</strong>:</p>
<pre><code>apple
banana
cherry
date</code></pre>

<h3>Sort numerically</h3>
<p>Input:</p>
<pre><code>100 items
3 items
42 items
7 items</code></pre>
<p>After clicking <strong>Sort Numeric</strong>:</p>
<pre><code>3 items
7 items
42 items
100 items</code></pre>

<h3>Combine operations</h3>
<p>You can chain operations: first <strong>Trim Lines</strong> to clean whitespace, then <strong>Remove Duplicates</strong>, then <strong>Sort A→Z</strong>. Copy the output back to input between steps or use the output directly.</p>
