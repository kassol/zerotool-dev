<h2>Slug Rules</h2>

A well-formed URL slug follows these conventions:

- Lowercase letters only (recommended for consistency)
- Words separated by hyphens or underscores
- No spaces, punctuation, or special characters
- No leading or trailing separators

```
Input:  "Hello World! Ça va?"
Slug:   "hello-world-ca-va"
```
