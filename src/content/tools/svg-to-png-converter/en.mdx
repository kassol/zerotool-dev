<h2>Why Convert SVG to PNG?</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Compatibility:</strong> PNG is universally supported across browsers, apps, and document editors that may not render SVG.</li>
  <li><strong>Social media:</strong> Platforms like Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Slack require raster images for uploads.</li>
  <li><strong>Fixed dimensions:</strong> PNG snapshots an SVG at a specific resolution, preventing unintended scaling.</li>
  <li><strong>Email clients:</strong> Most email clients do not support inline SVG; PNG is the safe choice.</li>
</ul>
