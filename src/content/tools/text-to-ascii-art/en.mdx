<h2>Text to ASCII Art Online</h2>
<p>Turn plain text into large ASCII banners directly in your browser. Choose a FIGlet font, preview the output instantly, and copy the result for a README, terminal welcome message, Discord post, or code comment.</p>

<h2>How to Use</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Type or paste text in the <strong>Input Text</strong> field — up to 80 characters.</li>
  <li>Select a <strong>Font</strong> from the dropdown to change the ASCII art style.</li>
  <li>The output updates automatically. Click <strong>Copy</strong> to copy the result.</li>
</ol>
